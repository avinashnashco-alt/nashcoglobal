About Nashco Global

Diversified Excellence Across the GCC Since 1994

Our Story

Founded in 1994 in Dubai, Nashco Global has expanded to a multinational group with operations in industrial plastic manufacturing (injection moulding), automotive sales/imports/exports, spare parts distribution, HFC refrigerant distribution, and general trading. Our USP: Audit-ready, integrated services for compliance and efficiency across UAE, Oman, KSA, and beyond.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparent operations with full compliance.
  • Innovation: Sustainable tech for market adaptation.
  • Reliability: Robust supply chains in GCC.
